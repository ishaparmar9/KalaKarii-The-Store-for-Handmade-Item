We know you want to know how your Handmade Products are made

TIMELESS DESIGNS

All of our products are designed such that they remain appealing through the passage of time and can even be passed down from generation to the next.

QUALITY

All our products handmade and are made up of natural fibers but we ensure that the quality we deliver be nothing less than what can be expected of industrial goods if not better.

ECO-FRIENDLY

The core of all our design though is to create eco-friendly products that our users can use to replace plastic products for everyday use.

HOUSEHOLD INDUSTRY

All our products are made by artisans from the comfort of their homes. They work a few hours a day without disrupting their day-to-day life in the villages while earning a sustainable income.

About Us

KalaKarii - the store of handmade products...Here we promote the skills of the local artisans. As per the number and type of orders, we give them contract and thus we connect huge number of artisans be it from West Bengal, Gujarat, Tamil Nadu, Bihar, Punjab, Jammu and many more...We encouraged our Artisans to explore the market and go to the Exhibitions to learn new things and learn about New Products. We support our Artisans to enter and thrive in the Online market.
We support our Artisans in Reviving traditional designs, organizing Exhibitions and workshops to grow a healthy relationship between the Craftspeople and customer.
KalaKarii is a Non-Government Organization that always motivates Craftspeople to follow fair trade practices. We don’t use child labour and always promote a healthy work environment.
